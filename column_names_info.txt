CRIM (Crime Rate): The per capita crime rate by town.

ZN (Residential Zone): The proportion of residential land zoned for large lots.

INDUS (Industrial Zone): The proportion of non-retail business acres per town.

CHAS (Charles River dummy variable): 1 if the tract bounds the river; 0 otherwise.

NOX (Nitric Oxides Concentration): Nitric oxides concentration (parts per 10 million).

RM (Average Number of Rooms): The average number of rooms per dwelling.

AGE (Proportion of Owner-Occupied Units Built Before 1940): The proportion of owner-occupied units built prior to 1940.

DIS (Weighted Distances to Employment Centers): The weighted distances to five Boston employment centers.

RAD (Accessibility to Highways): Index of accessibility to radial highways.

TAX (Property Tax Rate): Full-value property tax rate per $10,000.

PTRATIO (Pupil-teacher Ratio): Pupil-teacher ratio by town.

B (Proportion of Black Residents): 1000(Bk - 0.63)^2 where Bk is the proportion of Black residents by town.

LSTAT (Percentage of Lower Status of the Population): Percentage of the population with lower socioeconomic status.
